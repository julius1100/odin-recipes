Miso soup

Ingredients

  • 4 cups dashi broth (or water)
  • 3 tablespoons miso paste (white or red)
  • 1/2 cup tofu, cubed

Steps

  1. In a pot, bring the dashi broth to a simmer over medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the miso paste with a ladle of hot broth until smooth.
  3. Add the miso mixture back into the pot and stir well.
  4. Add the cubed tofu and let it heat through for about 2-3 minutes.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with sliced green onions if desired.
